Understanding the Urinary System

The urinary system, also known as the renal system, produces, stores, and eliminates urine. It includes the kidneys, ureters, bladder, and urethra. Click on different organs in the model to learn more about how they work together to remove waste from your body.

Welcome to the Urinary System Explorer

Click on any organ in the diagram to learn about its structure and function in the urinary system. The urinary system is essential for removing waste from your body and maintaining proper balance of water and minerals.
