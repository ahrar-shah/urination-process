Welcome to the Urinary System Explorer!

Click on any organ in the 3D model to learn about its structure and function.

The urinary system is responsible for removing waste from the blood and regulating fluid balance in the body.

It consists of:

  • Two kidneys
  • Two ureters
  • One urinary bladder
  • One urethra
